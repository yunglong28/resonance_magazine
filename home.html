Our Issues

Issue #1: Music Streaming Economy

This issue is dedicated to music streaming economy and its relation to digital technologies. Andy Cush from Pitchfork introduces us to the fight between musicians and Spotify: the colossus is reducing its payments to artist, making it more difficult for independent musicians to survive. Then, we propose a great think-piece by Baldr Eldursson, written for the now dead underground-cult blog TinyMixtapes. It is a reflection on how our listening's habits changed with the advent of pervasive digital technology in the last ten years: this is an original, provocative and unique piece as in the style of TinyMixtapes. Our last article looks at the future with an optimist perspective: NFTs and similar crypto-technology will probably shock the music industry in the next future. According to Mertens, they could also offer a way for implementing a more sustainable system of revenues, beyond the power of streaming platforms.
